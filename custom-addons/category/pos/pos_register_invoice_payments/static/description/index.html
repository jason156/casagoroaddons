POS Register Invoice Payments

This module allows the seller to view ,register or Unreconciled invoice payments in POS. Payment of invoice can be done by customer's available Outstanding credits or as well as manual payment.

POS Register Invoice Payments

With the help of this module, POS User has an option to view, Unreconciled or register invoice payment in running Point Of Sale Session. This module is good for scenario if the seller wishes to register invoice payment in POS, he can do so by our module. Seller is facilitated with some interesting features:

  • Register Invoice Payment through Outstanding Credits
  • Register Invoice Payment Manually
  • Unreconciled Invoice Payments

Features
  • Seller can view the list of invoices associated with his customers directly from a running POS session.
  • Seller can register invoice payments as well as view invoice payments in running POS Session.
  • Seller can Unreconciled payments of invoice in POS.
  • Seller can register invoice payment either manually or by Outstanding credits of associated customer.
  • POS Session View

POS Register Invoice Payments

View Customer Invoices
Register or View Invoice Payments
Register Payment by Customer's Outstanding Credits

Seller can select Outstanding Credits tab to register payment by using customer available Outstanding credits

Register Payment Manually

In case customer does'nt have Outstanding credits or wishes to register payment manually, then select Register Payment tab, fill details, select payment method and click validate button to register invoice payment

View & Unreconciled Invoice Payments

Seller can view invoice payments as well as Unreconciled payments in POS. Select Unreconciled tab to view invoice payments and click on Unreconciled button to Unreconciled payment.
