All in One Product Bundle Base

Do you want to make a combo of several products? This module is useful to make a pack of products in the point of sale, sales, purchase, inventory & invoice. You can generate a product bundle for selling multi-products at once. You can make a bunch of several products easily. cheers!

In the product tick "Is Bundled ?" to get the "Pack/Bundle Products" tab. You can count the total sales price of a bundle from the button "Compute Bundle Price".

You can add products in the "Pack/Bundle Products" tab.

If you want to count the sales price of a bundle then click the button "Compute Bundle Price".
